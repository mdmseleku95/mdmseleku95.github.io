Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

We collaborate with global stakeholders to promote leadership and research in various fields that
impact on society and its progressive development. We use our expertise, skills, experience, and
knowledge to develop leaders and empower them to make informed decisions.

Vision

As a world class-leader for leadership development, research and innovation, we are the most
global resourceful leadership and research institute.

Values

• Collaboration
• Credibility
• Integrity
• Leadership

Our Aim

We Aim To


• Promote research
• Empower leaders in public entities (all spheres of government and in the private sector,
  to become better leaders and make informed decisions
• Share leadership and research best practices
• Work with entities including public, private and non-governmental organizations to
  undertake meaningful research, share community empowering and research results.
• Support and inform the development of evidence-based policies and programmes
• Empower business leaders, government authorities and mangers to make informed
  decision
• Promote leadership development, with emphasis on strategic leadership
• Develop leadership of various professionals
• Inspire practitioners to participate in meaningful research activities
• Promote collaboration in research
• Capacitate and empower professionals to conduct publishable research
• Identify research topics that will promote professions
• Use leadership and research as a platform for positive social change
• Mentor and coach scholars in leadership and research
• Develop scholars for positive social change
• Recognize scholars, practitioners, individuals, and organizations that make a positive
  social impact in various fields and in the society.
• Make research materials and reports available and accessible.
• Support young researchers: help ensure that practitioners at the beginning of their career
  are highly creative and original

Dr Sibongiseni Myeni

Dr Sibongiseni Myeni is a University of Zululand alumnus, University of KwaZulu-Natal alumnus, Nelson Mandela University alumnus, and Walden University alumnus. After completing his Doctor of Business Administration, together with Dr Ngcobo, formally launched the Institute for Leadership and Research, in pursuit of their mission as lifelong scholars, learners, and implementers of positive social change.

Dr Ntombenhle Ngcobo

Dr Ngcobo is a University of Natal alumnus, University of the Witwatersrand alumnus, and Nelson Mandela University alumnus. Dr Ngcobo is a medical doctor with post graduate training in occupational health, public health and bioethics. She has Master of Science in Medicine in the field of Bioethics and Health Law; and a Master of Business Administration.
